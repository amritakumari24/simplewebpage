ITI of Chhattisgarh plays an important role in providing technical and vocational education to students across the state. It focuses on skill based training in trades such as electrician, fitter, welder, computer operator, and mechanic. The institute helps students gain practical knowledge along with basic theory, making them job ready for industries and self employment. ITI of CG supports government initiatives like Skill India by improving employability among youth. With experienced instructors, workshops, and industry oriented curriculum, it creates opportunities for students to build strong careers and contribute to economic development in the modern competitive technical education environment nationwide growth.
